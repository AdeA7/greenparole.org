About Green Parole

Founded in 2009, Green Parole emerged from a simple yet profound realization that true national progress is rooted in an engaged, educated, and empowered citizenry. For over a decade, we have dedicated ourselves to a singular mission: to educate and empower young Nigerians through citizenship education and civic engagement. Our goal is to inspire a new generation of leaders and active participants who understand responsibilities, and can become the architects of their own future and the guardians of our collective destiny.

Our core philosophy is centered on the principle of responsible citizenship. We equip young Nigerians with the knowledge they need to understand government expectations, policies, and the frameworks of governance. By demystifying the political process, we empower them to move from passive observers to active stakeholders. This education is a vital step toward creating a more transparent and accountable society, where citizens are not just recipients of services, but active participants in demanding good governance and holding their leaders accountable. We teach them to be informed voters, articulate advocates, and community problem-solvers.

Green Parole is providing a platform for participants to be change ambassadors. We foster a culture of leadership where young people are encouraged to be beacons of hope and positive influence in their communities. We are promoting and rewarding values of integrity, empathy, and service. This ambassadorship extends beyond formal roles; it's a way of life that encourages them to carry the mission of empowerment and civic duty into every facet of their lives.
